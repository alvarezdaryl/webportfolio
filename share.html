My 20th Birthday

Celebrated my 20th Birthday last 12th of July 2017 at Enchanted Kingdom with my squad! This was actually planned and was supposed December 2015, but then, nauwi sa Drawing. And I'm honestly pissed that time. Then I planned this again November 2016 so its 7 month preparation, ang tagal no? Ganto kase, some of us hindi pinapayagang gumala ng malayo so i though that tutal kapag may birthday sameng lima it's a priviledge for us 5 na payagang gumala kahit saan so i grab this opportunity na sa birthday ko maganap etong matagal ko nang binabalak simula pa nung 2nd year college pa ko.

and now here we at! Natupad na nga tong matagal ko nang binalak! This was honestly my birthday wish. Na maicelebrate ko birthday ko sa amuesment park (E.K specifically) i'm a bit emotional whilst writing this coz my squad, they loved me so much na igrant tong wish na to saken and they really prepare this. Biruin mo 7 months tsumempo makapagpaalam na mag EEK lang kame huhu i cri mga bes.

but then let's see my ootd naman nung nag EK kame. Goiz casual type lang tayo para kumportable kumilos, we're not here to be conscious of what we look like and "dress to kill". I'm here to have fun! And beside, i slayed my casual hello! Like I always damn do! Simple pero may dating! Hahaha ok nag bubuhat na naman ako ng bangko ko, but yea forgive me for being egotistic and ggs.
